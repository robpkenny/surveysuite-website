Survey Management Software for Independent Surveyors

Survey Suite helps residential surveyors spend less time on administration and more time surveying. It brings together client management, job tracking, inspections, report delivery and workflow automation in one simple platform.

Making surveying simple

Survey Suite is being developed for UK residential surveyors who want a clearer, more efficient way to manage their surveying workload from first enquiry through to report delivery.

The aim is simple: less duplicated admin, fewer disconnected systems and more time for surveying.

Planned Survey Suite tools

Survey CRM

Manage clients, agents, referrers, properties, instructions and job stages in one structured system.

Inspection Management

Keep track of inspections, access notes, appointments, survey types and job progress from enquiry to completion.

Report Delivery

Support modern report workflows, including client portals, digital delivery and future HTML5 report options.

Workflow Automation

Reduce repetitive admin with automated reminders, stage progression, document handling and follow-up tasks.

Client Portal

Give clients a clear place to access documents, updates and completed survey outputs securely.

Mobile Surveyor App

Support surveyors in the field with job information, site notes and mobile-first inspection workflows.
